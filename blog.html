WELCOME

Hi I'm Eva Salomon Geiger , a.k.a. Siren Cake Studio. I first fell in love with cake while spending a semester in college at the Marchutz School of Painting in France. Instead of spending extra time in the studio, I spent my days exploring the patisseries that lined every street. My art from that point forward had a continuous theme: food.

After graduating with a degree in sculpture and edible-art history, I enrolled at the prestigious French Pastry School, in Chicago. From the world-renowned faculty I learned the art of patisserie: from ice cream and petits fours to bread and chocolates.

It quickly became clear that my talents lay in cake decorating, where art and dessert combine. From there I went on to work with some of the finest cake designers in Chicago, including Mary Winslow of Take the Cake and Food Network Challenge winners Sarah Levy and The Cakegirls.

During my time in Chicago, I also trained at the Wilton School of Decorating and completed the French Pastry Schoolâ€™s decorator certification program with honors. These classes were instructed by some of my cake heroes including Colette Peters, Nicholas Lodge, and Mark Seaman.

I have since returned to New England. Inspired by the beauty of the Maine Coast and the quality of the Portland food scene, I continue to create sinfully delicious cakes in beautiful custom designs. I was previously executive pastry chef at the Black Tie Company, in Portland.
